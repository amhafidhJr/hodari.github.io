About Us

Our mission is to strengthen and streamline data and systems frameworks while simultaneously upskilling ICT users on platforms that support evidence based management of health and other sectors through data science capacity building, consultation, research and innovation.

Learn More

The name Hodari is an acronym for Health and other Data Analytics, Research and Informatics Initiatives and is a Tanzanian advocacy and operational Non-Governmental Organization founded this year in Zanzibar. Our primary objective is to establish an informatics culture in institutions that serve the general public.

HODARI Initiative envisions the Tanzanian public and private sector being data literate, where health and other sectors are managed through thriving informatics ecosystems for the public good.
