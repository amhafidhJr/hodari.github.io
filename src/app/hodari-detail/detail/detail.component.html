About Us

Who we are?

The name Hodari is an acronym for Health and other Data Analytics, Research and Informatics Initiatives and is a Tanzanian advocacy and operational Non-Governmental Organization founded this year in Zanzibar. Our primary objective is to establish an informatics culture in institutions that serve the general public.

Background

HODARI Initiative is a national advocacy and operational Non-Governmental Organization; whose primary objective is to establish informatics culture in institutions that serve the public. There is a global demand to enforce informatics culture and enhance productivity, however this requires combined effort from both governmental and non-governmental organizations. Informatics is the key to inform policy makers, implementers, and users of information to obtain main objectives through data use to tackle major challenges and adequately allocating the limited resources we have. These transformations need to realign our information sources, workforce training and multiple demonstrations though research and Innovations. We acknowledge the efforts of our governments to grow ICT, its application lies in proper integration with information users and Informatics is the bridge. HODARI Initiative believes in capitalizing the ICT potential to deepen usability and unleash the potential of information in our health sector and beyond.
